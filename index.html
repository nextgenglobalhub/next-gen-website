Who can apply for this cohort?
This cohort has been curated for outperforming individuals who love taking up new challenges and solve problems. You will be a good fit if:
  • You have beginner level experience in opensource and are looking to upskill or participate in Open Source Program.
  • You have started contributing in baisc opensource project but do not know how to start contributing in large code base projects and looking for a peer group & contributors know how to crush this.
  • You're a open source contributor who is looking for a like-minded community of passionate folks, build a network and grow!
What is the application process?
Fill your details on this page and keep a look at your inbox as we send you an email with further details. That's everything needed from your end for registration. Upon selection, you'll receive a confirmation email before the cohort starts around late Feburary.
How much does this cohort cost?
We are a volunteer-driven community of like-minded individuals connected with the shared vision of helping people progress in their career. We have set the price for our this program as Rs 0.
What access do I get as a participant?
As a participant you get access to:
  • 3/4-week program & curriculum from the phenomenal industry experts. Engagement activities like breakout sessions, icebreakers, group case studies, review sessions & much more.
  • A close knit community of talented individuals to grow and learn together.
  • Build deep meaningful relationships with exceptional individuals from different backgrounds.
  • Access to the entire nextgen ecosystem: Grab Chai, Web3 , Generative AI, Product Teardowns, Learn PM & much more.
How much time do I need to commit every week?
Our curriculum is part-time friendly, so you are expected to spend 4-5 hours per week but the more time you will spend in networking & engaging, the more you will gain.
